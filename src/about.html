Meet our Founder

Edite Jespere

Edite is a chartered accountant with a proven track record in delivering comprehensive accounting and financial management services to both individuals and businesses. With extensive experience in financial reporting, tax advisory, and risk management, her expertise has contributed to the success of several major projects.

In addition to her work with established businesses, Edite has significant experience supporting start-ups, offering guidance in financial modelling, systems implementation, and risk management. As the head of Fincept's team of accountants, she ensures that clients receive the best value from expert accounting services.

Our Mission

At Fincept, our mission is to empower UK start-ups and SMEs with comprehensive, reliable, and tailored accounting, tax, and financial management services. We aim to simplify financial complexities, enabling our clients to focus on growth and innovation, while we ensure their financial health and compliance.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become the trusted financial partner for start-ups and SMEs across the UK, driving sustainable growth through expert accounting, strategic financial planning, and insightful management. We aspire to be the go-to firm that helps businesses thrive by delivering exceptional value, innovation, and financial clarity.
