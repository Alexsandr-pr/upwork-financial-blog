How it works

01

Free Consultation

We start with a free consultation to understand your business's financial goals and accounting requirements.  During this session, we’ll discuss your current accounting processes, the systems you have in place, and your future objectives.

02

Business Analysis

After the meeting, our team will analyse how we can best support your business and provide you with a tailored accounting package designed specifically for your needs.

03

Accounting Set-up

Once we begin, if you're a new business, we will help you set up an accounting system and explain all financial deliverables, as well as how we will manage them. If you're an existing business switching from a different accountant, we will ensure a smooth and seamless transition.

04

Ongoing Support

We provide unlimited ongoing support. If you have any questions or need assistance at any point, our team is always available to help. Whether it’s clarification on a process or advice on a financial decision, we are here to ensure you receive the guidance and support you need, whenever you need it.

Accounting Solutions for your Start-up

Getting your finances right from the very beginning is crucial for long-term success. We assist start-ups in understanding and meeting their financial and accounting requirements, ensuring full compliance with regulations from the very start.

In addition, we offer flexible CFO services tailored to your business needs. Whether you need a few hours a week or a month for financial planning, capital structure guidance, or risk management, we’re here to help. There’s no long-term commitment—our CFO can come on board to address immediate challenges, optimise your financial processes, and then transition responsibilities back to the business owners once everything is running smoothly.

We also assist with system implementation and automation wherever possible, ensuring that start-up owners can maximise their efficiency and focus their time on growing the business.
